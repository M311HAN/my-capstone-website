Hobbies

Football

I loved spending hours watching football and every weekend I would watch my favourite football team play with my dad.

I played for a football team called Galatasaray. On my 15th birthday, I scored 4 goals and took my team to the semi-finals.

Drawing

I spent most afternoons drawing my favourite cartoon characters and drawing my parents while they sat and watched TV.

My dad used to bring me crayons and paper every time he came home from work.

Toys

Legos

Playing with legos was my favourite activity. I always wanted to build the biggest and coolest creations like cars and tall buildings.

My parents bought me a new Lego set every birthday and my dad would help build projects with me.

Bicycle

My bicycle was my new adventure and freedom. I would ride it around the neighbourhood with my friends, and we would all make our way to the skatepark.

One day I fell off my bike, and my dad had to fix it for me. He told me that falling off will only make you better and better.
